ABOUT

I am a PhD student in Electronic Engineering at the University of Rome "Tor Vergata". I am working in the Network Programmability Laboratory, under the supervision of Prof. Giuseppe Bianchi, and I am involved in many industry-related projects, such as EU-H2020 on stateful network data planes (BEBA, SUPERFLUIDITY, 5G-Picture) and Cisco grants about Cisco open-source SW router (Vector Packet Processor - VPP).

Before the PhD, Valerio worked as a Researcher at CNIT for 2-years and as a R&D engineer at Telecom ParisTech for 1-year. His research interests are focused on Network Function Virtualization (NFV) and Software Defined Networking (SDN) topics, in particular related on high speed network data planes and traffic monitoring.
